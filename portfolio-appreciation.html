We want to help individuals share their gratitude! Studies show that people who deliberately practice appreciation through acts of kindness, daily reflection, and expressions of gratitude rate their lives as more enjoyable than average.

Here at Appreciation Notes, we know that busy lives can cause moments of thanks to get lost in the shuffle. Our app allows users to jot down notes and share them with those they care about. From a coworker doing a great job, to a significant other who makes your day brighter, we want to help people connect with those they appreciate and share in their thanks.
